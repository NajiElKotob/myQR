# myQR — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

## Data Collection
myQR does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.
All data including saved URLs, labels, and notes is stored 
exclusively on your local device using SQLite.

## Internet Access
myQR does not connect to the internet at any time.
No data is sent to any server.

## Third-Party Services
myQR does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, 
or tracking services.
